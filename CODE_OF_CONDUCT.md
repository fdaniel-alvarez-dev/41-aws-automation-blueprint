# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent; communicate clearly.
- Focus on what’s best for the community and the work.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open an issue with context.
